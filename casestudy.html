Poppi is a modern, culture-forward beverage brand built for digital-first audiences. While the brand had strong momentum, scaling reach through traditional channels was becoming increasingly expensive and less effective at capturing authentic engagement.

Poppi needed a way to:

  • Stay culturally relevant

  • Activate creators at scale

  • Turn UGC into a measurable performance channel

Without adding massive production overhead.

The Challenge

Diminishing Returns From Traditional Media

Poppi’s media mix leaned heavily on paid placements and high-production brand campaigns. While visually polished, these assets struggled to match the authenticity and velocity of creator-driven content on platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

Key challenges included:

  • High cost per impression

  • Limited creative iteration speed

  • Creator content that couldn’t scale fast enough

  • Difficulty sustaining always-on social presence

Poppi needed volume, speed, and authenticity—without sacrificing quality.

The Solution

TwinTone enabled Poppi to launch a scalable, AI-driven UGC engine—blending real creator storytelling with AI-assisted production to dramatically increase output and experimentation.

Using TwinTone, Poppi was able to:

  • Generate creator-style videos at scale

  • Rapidly iterate messaging, hooks, and formats

  • Activate experiential content without production bottlenecks

  • Maintain brand consistency across hundreds of assets

The result was a flexible content system designed for feed velocity, not one-off campaigns.

TwinTone's AI video maker empowered Poppi with instant video creation.

Results

Massive Reach at Lower Creative Friction

With TwinTone powering UGC production and testing, Poppi achieved:

  • 63M+ total impressions

  • 218M creator-driven reach

  • Sustained engagement across platforms

  • A repeatable system for launching creator-led campaigns


The data validated a critical insight: UGC doesn’t just complement traditional media—it can outperform it.

Impact

From Campaigns to a Scalable Growth Engine

TwinTone helped Poppi move From One-off creator campaigns to an always-on, scalable UGC system

Key outcomes:

  • Higher engagement through authentic storytelling

  • Faster creative iteration without agency bottlenecks

  • Lower dependency on high-cost traditional media

  • A repeatable playbook for future launches
